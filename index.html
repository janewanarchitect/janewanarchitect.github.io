Profile

Jane is a licensed architect with over a decade of experience delivering complex, cross-sector projects. Her work spans a broad range of project types and scales, including aviation, transportation, civic facilities, and private residences.

Actively engaged in the profession, she serves as a Director at AIA Silicon Valley, a Subject Matter Expert for the California Architects Board, and an NCARB Committee Member. Her projects have received award from AIA California and have been featured in Metropolis magazine.

Jane is committed to uncovering the unique potential of every project, achieving design excellence grounded in clarity, value, and lasting impact.
